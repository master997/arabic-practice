# Inbox — paste this week's tutor words here

Drop what you learned this week below the line, **one word or phrase per line**.
Be as rough as you like — Arabic alone, or Arabic then a loose English meaning.
Separators don't matter (space, dash, tab, `|` all fine):

```
عايز        I want
بكام؟       how much?
لسه بدري    it's still early
الاهرامات
```

Then tell Claude: **"process my inbox."**

Claude will, for each new line: write clean Egyptian pronunciation (`3`=ع, `'`=hamza/ق,
`g`=ج), find the three-letter root, add a natural example sentence and topic tags,
skip anything already in the app, append the finished cards to the app's **My words**
store, and clear the processed lines from below. Anything ambiguous is left here with a
note rather than guessed.
